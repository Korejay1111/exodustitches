About Exodus Stitches

Based in the heart of Ibadan, Exodus Stitches has been a beacon of Nigerian fashion excellence for over a decade. We specialize in creating stunning outfits that celebrate our rich Yoruba heritage while embracing contemporary style.

Our commitment to neat finishing, attention to detail, and customer satisfaction has made us the go-to fashion house for discerning clients across Nigeria and beyond. Every stitch tells a story of craftsmanship, creativity, and cultural pride.

Size Guide

Measurement Guide

Follow our simple guide to take accurate measurements for your perfect fit.

1
Bust Measurement

Measure around the fullest part of your bust, keeping the tape straight across your back.

2
Waist Measurement

Measure around your natural waistline, usually the smallest part of your waist.

3
Hip Measurement

Measure around the fullest part of your hips, approximately 7-8 inches below your waist.

4
Length Measurement

Measure from shoulder to desired hem length for dresses, or waist to ankle for skirts/trousers.

5
Shoulder Width

Measure from shoulder tip to shoulder tip across the back.

6
Arm Length

Measure from shoulder to wrist for sleeve length.
